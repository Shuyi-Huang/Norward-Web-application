NORWARD PTY LTD

ELECTRICAL CONTRACTORS, TERRITORY WIDE


Norward Pty Ltd is locally owned and operated. We provide industrial Electrical and Instrumentation services to the Utilities, Mining, Petrochemical, Agricultural and Defence sectors, whilst also offering Marine Electrical Services tailored for the unique requirements of the Northern Territory maritime industry.

Delivering specialised solutions within four core focuses – Construction, Commissioning, Maintenance and Marine, we operate the breadth of the Northern Territory, servicing some of the most remote locations in Australia. Since our establishment in 2021, Norward Pty Ltd has consistently delivered successful project outcomes, regardless of scale, complexity or location. Our growing team possesses the necessary skills and experience, ensuring reliable and professional project completion.

We take pride in our commitment to meeting project deadlines, managing budget constraints and upholding high-quality standards, showcasing our reliability and professionalism in every project undertaken.

Norward Pty Ltd operates within the framework of an Environmental, Health and Safety Management System which forms part of our Policy and Procedures
